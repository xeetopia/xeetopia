The Journey
MY STORY
Still editing. Still going.
"It wasn't easy. But nothing worth having ever is — and I'm still here."

Xeet is a self-taught video editor and creator from West Bengal, India. The path here wasn't paved or planned — it was scraped together through hard times, setbacks, and moments where giving up felt like the only option. There were days when resources were scarce, support was absent, and progress felt invisible. But the timeline was always open. The edit never stopped.

Every skill learned was self-taught. Every project was a fight to get better. The grind wasn't glamorous — it was quiet, lonely work at odd hours, fueled by a stubborn belief that things could be made beautiful out of nothing. If you've ever felt behind, overwhelmed, or like the odds are stacked against you — this story might feel familiar. And if it does, know this: it's possible to keep going. The edit isn't over.
